Care Services

E Care Locator

Eligible seniors can apply for subsidised care services by getting their Medical Social Workers, attending doctors or Social Workers to help put up a referral and application to Agency For Integrated Care (AIC). The referral team at AIC will then get in touch with some recommendations based on what you need.

You can use the E-care Locator to find out more about services near you and share your preferences during the application process.
